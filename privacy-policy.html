Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

Crypto Ledger-Tracker Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data, including your crypto transactions, prices, and charts, is stored locally on your device.

We do not upload your data to any servers or cloud services. Your information remains on your device and is accessible only to you.

We do not share your data with third parties because we do not collect any data from you.

Crypto Ledger-Tracker Pro does not request any permissions that access your personal information, such as location, contacts, or camera.
